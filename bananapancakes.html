What You'll Need:

Quantity/Amount: Ingredients:
2 Beaten Eggs
2 Mashed Bananas
1 Cup Flour
1 Tablespoon Coconut Oil

What to do:

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Mix together the bananas with the eggs and slowly add the flour.
  3. Pour desire amount of mixture onto heated pan.
  4. Wait for slight bubbling on top, flip pancake to opposite side.
  5. Flip onto a cooling rack when ready and eat plain or with your favorite toppings.
