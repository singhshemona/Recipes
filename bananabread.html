Banana Bread

What You'll Need:

Quantity/Amount: Ingredient:
2 Cups Flour
1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Cup Butter
3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
2 Beaten Eggs
2 1/3 Mashed Overripe Bananas

What to do:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
    Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  4. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
    Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes,
    until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
  6. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
