What You'll Need:

Quantity/Amount: Ingredient:
3 Cups Cold Milk
2 Packets Vanilla Flavored Instant Pudding
30 Vanilla Wafers
3 Sliced Bananas
1 Tub Whipped Cream (thawed)

What to do:

  1. Beat pudding mixes and milk with whisk 2 min. Let stand 5 min.
  2. Arrange half the wafers on bottom and up side of 2-qt. serving bowl;
    top with layers of half each of the banana slices and pudding.
    Repeat all layers.
  3. Cover with 1/2 inch of whipped cream.
  4. Refrigerate 3 hours.
