What You'll Need:

Quantity/Amount: Ingredient:
1, 6 Ounce Container Greek Yogurt
2 Tablespoons Peanut Butter
1-2 Tablespoons Sweetener of Choice
1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
1/2 Teaspoon Chocolate Chips

What to do:

  1. In a small-medium bowl, whisk together everything except
    the chocolate chips until smooth.
  2. Refrigerate until ready to eat.
  3. Top with chocolate chips.
